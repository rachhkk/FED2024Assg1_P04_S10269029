About

Aespa is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment. The group debuted in November 2020 and has quickly become known for their unique concept, blending virtual and real worlds through avatars, called "æ", and their powerful music. They released their newest album "Whiplash" on October 21 2024.
